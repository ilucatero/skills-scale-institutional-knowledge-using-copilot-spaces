# OctoAcme Personas

This document defines typical roles and responsibilities used in OctoAcme project docs and exercises.

---

## Developers

### Role Summary
Developers design, build, test, and deliver software components. They collaborate with product and project leads to implement features that meet acceptance criteria and quality standards.

### Responsibilities
- Implement features and fixes to meet acceptance criteria
- Write and maintain tests and documentation
- Participate in design and code reviews
- Assist in estimating and planning work
- Help identify technical risks and propose mitigations

### Goals
- Deliver reliable, maintainable code
- Reduce cycle time from idea to production
- Maintain high test coverage and observability

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning
- PR descriptions and code review comments
- Technical design docs when needed

---

## Product Managers

### Role Summary
Product Managers define what should be built to deliver customer and business value. They own the product vision, prioritize the backlog, and measure outcomes.

### Responsibilities
- Define problem statements and success metrics
- Prioritize the roadmap and backlog
- Collaborate with stakeholders and engineering on trade-offs
- Validate solutions through user research and metrics

### Goals
- Maximize customer value and impact
- Make clear, data-driven prioritization decisions
- Ensure product-market fit and usability

### Typical Communication
- Weekly alignment with PM and engineering leads
- Roadmap updates and stakeholder briefings
- Acceptance criteria and feature specs

---

## Project Managers

### Role Summary
Project Managers coordinate delivery activities, manage schedules, risks, and communications. They enable the team to deliver on commitments efficiently.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain project plans and timelines
- Manage risks, dependencies, and resource constraints
- Facilitate meetings (kickoff, planning, retrospectives)
- Ensure consistent project documentation and status reporting
- Coordinate cross-team and stakeholder communication

### Goals
- Deliver projects on time and within scope
- Minimize unplanned work and escalations
- Maintain transparency and alignment across stakeholders

### Typical Communication
- Weekly status updates and stakeholder reports
- Risk registers and decision logs
- Coordination via project boards and meeting facilitation

---

## UX/UI Designer

### Role Summary
Ensures the product is usable, accessible, and visually aligned with user and business needs.

### Responsibilities
- Design wireframes, mockups, and user flows
- Conduct user research and usability testing
- Collaborate closely with Product Managers and Developers to iterate on designs
- Ensure accessibility standards are met

### Goals
- Enhance usability and user satisfaction
- Ensure designs align with product vision and accessibility standards
- Reduce design rework through early validation

### Interaction with existing roles
- **Product Manager**: Partners on feature requirements and user prioritization
- **Developers**: Collaborates during implementation for technical feasibility
- **Stakeholders**: Gathers feedback and validates design decisions
- **QA/Testing**: Works together on usability and acceptance testing

### Typical Communication
- Design reviews with Product Manager and Developers
- Usability research findings and recommendations
- Design specifications and component documentation

---

## Business Analyst

### Role Summary
Bridges the gap between business needs and technical implementation, clarifying requirements and documenting workflows.

### Responsibilities
- Elicit and document requirements from stakeholders
- Analyze process gaps and suggest improvements
- Translate business needs into actionable acceptance criteria
- Document workflows and create process maps
- Support requirement clarifications during development

### Goals
- Ensure clarity and completeness of requirements
- Support alignment between business goals and delivery
- Reduce rework due to unclear or incomplete specifications
- Improve process efficiency

### Interaction with existing roles
- **Product Manager**: Collaborates on prioritization and requirement validation
- **Project Manager**: Provides requirement clarity during planning and execution
- **Developers**: Answers questions and clarifies acceptance criteria
- **Stakeholders**: Gathers initial requirements and validates solutions

### Typical Communication
- Requirement documentation and specifications
- Process flow diagrams and workflow documentation
- Q&A sessions with developers and stakeholders
- Change request assessments

---

## Scrum Master

### Role Summary
Facilitates the Agile process, supports the team's progress, and removes impediments.

### Responsibilities
- Facilitate sprint ceremonies (planning, standups, reviews, retros)
- Track team health, velocity, and delivery metrics
- Coach the team on Agile best practices and continuous improvement
- Remove blockers and escalate impediments appropriately
- Maintain team focus on sprint goals and Definition of Done

### Goals
- Enable smooth, predictable team workflow
- Foster a culture of continuous improvement
- Maximize team productivity and morale
- Support consistent delivery cadence

### Interaction with existing roles
- **Project Manager**: Coordinates on schedule and dependency management
- **Developers**: Removes technical and process blockers
- **Product Manager**: Assists with backlog refinement and prioritization
- **Stakeholders**: Communicates team status and escalates business-impacting issues

### Typical Communication
- Sprint ceremonies facilitation notes
- Team health and velocity metrics
- Impediment and action item tracking
- Coaching and retrospective summaries

---

## QA/Testing

### Role Summary
Validates that features meet acceptance criteria and quality standards, ensuring reliability and user confidence in releases.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain test plans and test cases
- Perform manual and automated testing
- Validate acceptance criteria compliance
- Identify and document defects
- Participate in Definition of Done review

### Goals
- Deliver high-quality, reliable features
- Reduce production defects and incidents
- Ensure user confidence through thorough validation

### Interaction with existing roles
- **Product Manager**: Clarifies acceptance criteria and prioritization
- **Developers**: Communicates defects and collaborates on fixes
- **Business Analyst**: Validates requirements interpretation
- **Project Manager**: Updates on testing progress and blockers

### Typical Communication
- Test results and defect reports
- Test case documentation
- Quality metrics and trends
- Testing blockers and dependencies
